ANALYSIS

Specializing in Engineering Services for Aerospace, Automotive, Industrial Products & More

What do ATA customers value about our engineering solutions?

ATA customers value our cutting-edge tools and technological methods that help them fulfill project requirements more accurately and efficiently. They rely on our dedication to problem solving and our deep knowledge of regulatory and manufacturing requirements as well as current market and industry factors.

Where some engineering services companies send you one engineer, ATA assigns a cross-functional team, so your project benefits from the expertise of many different engineers, not just one. We work on a project basis, so you pay for support only as long as you need it. This approach lets you ramp up your resources to meet critical deadlines and eliminates downtime.

Aerospace

At Calcate, our engineers have extensive experience working in the aerospace industry, working for the Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEM) and large/small Tier 2 suppliers. We have experience working on fuselage, landing gear, seats and interiors, engines, control surfaces and more.
We have experience building models from scratch, and doing sophisticated analysis such as bird strike, engine blade-off, fuselage crash, seating dynamics, aircraft water ditching, etc.
When it comes to analysis in aerospace, we can assist you from initial design to certification.

Airframes

At Calcate, our engineers have worked on numerous small aircraft, electric vertical take off (Evtol) planes and drone programs from inception to first flight. We have sized and analysed components, assemblies and full aircrafts using a building block approach. Confidence is built in the modelling and materials by first performing coupon level analysis and testing, followed by element level, subcomponent and then full aircraft level analysis, testing and correlation.
We have sized by performing extensive detailed analysis on metallic and composite airframe structures to meet industry requirements for strength, fatigue and crash load cases. Our specialized knowledge and experience in analysing and test correlation includes:
Fuselage drop testing, fuselage bird strike , aircraft ditching, landing gear, battery protection, aircraft seats and occupant safety.
