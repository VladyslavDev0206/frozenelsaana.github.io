About Us

Calcate is a consulting firm in the field of engineering analysis founded in 2012. Our engineers have decades of analysis experience and we know how to optimise your designs to meet your requirements, whether in aerospace, automotive, industrial, marine, energy, bespoke or consumer products. Our clients range from start-ups looking to launch their first product to large companies who are leaders in their industry.

Calcate is a small company (less than 10 analysts) which enables us to be flexible and react quickly to customer needs. We are passionate about helping our clients identify their specific needs and using our analysis expertise to improve their products. We work with your engineers and provide analysis expertise to meet your deadlines, using your tools or new tools depending on your challenges. We do not sell or promote any software so we will always use the most appropriate and efficient approach.

Our talented team of engineers can help your team solve urgent technical challenges, while implement new analysis methods and testing using industry standard and leading edge software and methods.

We have a wide range of analysis expertise including hand calculations, FEA, dynamics, vibration, crash, impact, optimization, and analysis-test correlation.

Calcate can assess your products against requirements such as displacements, strength, durability, resonance, impact safety and thermal.
