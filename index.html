Analysis Experts

At Calcate, we work with your engineers and provide analysis expertise to meet your deadlines, using your tools or new tools depending on your challenges.

Our talented team of engineers can help your team solve urgent technical challenges, while implement new analysis methods and testing correlation.

We enable our customers to achieve higher performance in mechanical, structural, and aerospace products.

Our Disciplines
Conceptual & Detailed Design

Conceptual Design


  • product requirements are confirmed to clarify the problems be solved.
  • Potential solutions are brainstormed, listing the pros and cons of implementing them, the preferred design being chosen for further investigation in the detailed design stage.

Detailed Design


  • approved conceptual design is refined, and drawings, specifications and estimates are created.

Finite Element Analysis

We use a variety of techniques and software to evaluate components performance under different operational conditions and loads.

Our FEA consulting engineers have extensive education and experience in:

  • Structural Analysis
  • Fatigue, Fracture & Durability
  • Thermal
  • Dynamic & Vibration
  • Composites

Crash & Impact

Impact, shock and crash are events with short time duration that require the use of explicit analysis.

Common assessments include:

  • Product drop testing
  • Vehicle/Aircraft crash testing
  • Blast and ballistics
  • Accident analysis
  • Catastrophic failure cases
  • Impact protection

Our consulting engineers asses the structural integrity after impact.

Classical Hand Calculations

Classical hand calculations provide an extremely fast and simple option to evaluate simple structures & forces including:

  • Free Body diagrams to understand load path
  • Sizing simple structures
  • Analysis prior to CAD/FEA
  • Check validity of FEA models

We use hand calculations to size and evaluate structures like bolted joints, lugs, splines, etc.

Optimization

The goal in topology optimization is to optimise for factors such as loads, boundary conditions, constraints and material properties while attaining desirable performance including:

  • Stiffness-to-weight ratio
  • Strain energy to weight ratio
  • Natural frequency to weight ratio.

Optimization can be done during the initial stages of design or after.

Test - Analysis Correlation

Test - analysis correlation is done to minimize the difference between physical test results and FEA predictions.
The FEA model can be used to design structures once it is correlated to a physical test and there is confidence in its predictive capabilities.
Our expert analysts can assist you in:
  • preparation for upcoming test campaigns
  • choose load cells, strain gauge & camera locations, etc.
  • Correlate model with test data
